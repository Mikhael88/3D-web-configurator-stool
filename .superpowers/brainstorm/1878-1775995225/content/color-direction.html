Color Direction

Current palette is deep navy/charcoal (#0c0e12 bg, #bcc7de accent). Which direction for the new look?

A — Refine Current

Keep deep navy/dark base. Adjust accent from steel-blue to warmer gold/sand. More luxury, less cold.

B — Light & Coastal

Off-white/linen background, navy text, sand & slate accents. Bright, editorial, nautical.

C — Dark & Natural

Darker green-black base, sage/seafoam & warm linen accents. Organic, high-end outdoor.
